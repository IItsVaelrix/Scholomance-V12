# Shading & Lighting

How to create convincing form, depth, and material in limited palettes.

Professional shading is not about "pretty gradients" — it is about communicating surface, volume, and light with the fewest pixels possible.

## Core Concepts
- Form shadows vs. cast shadows
- Specular, rim, and bounce light in pixel constraints
- Material indication (metal, cloth, organic, energy, crystal)
- Limited palette shading strategies
- Avoiding "pillow shading" and muddiness
