# Fundamentals

The non-negotiable foundations (silhouette, readability, pixel logic, contrast).

This is the bedrock of the PixelBrain Library of Alexandria. Every professional pixel artist masters these before moving on to shading, color, or style.

## Core Principles
- **Silhouette**: The shape must read clearly even at 1-bit or tiny sizes.
- **Readability**: Every pixel must serve a purpose. No noise.
- **Pixel Logic**: Understand how pixels cluster, align, and avoid jaggies or "crawlies".
- **Contrast & Hierarchy**: Value separation before hue. Important elements must pop.
