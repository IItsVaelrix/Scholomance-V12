# Common Pitfalls

The specific mistakes that keep work looking amateur and exactly how to eliminate them.

This is the "brutally honest" section. Every veteran has seen these thousands of times.

## Frequent Offenders
- Fuzzy or "crawly" edges
- Pillow shading / inconsistent light direction
- Overuse of anti-aliasing or dither in the wrong places
- Weak silhouettes (especially on circular/energy forms like the Void Shield)
- Center elements that fight the overall design
- Palette choices that collapse at small sizes or in motion
- Inconsistent pixel density within a single piece

For each pitfall: why it happens, why it looks bad, and the exact corrective mindset + technique.
