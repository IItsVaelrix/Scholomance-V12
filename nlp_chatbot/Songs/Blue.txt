perpetual blue
staining my fingertips
spreading to those I touch
infecting those I love

tremendous pain
engulfing the senses
begging for reprieve
punishment ongoing
bleeding into my dreams
tortured while asleep
tortured while awake

broken pieces
strewn across the floor
pick and choose
what to keep
what to throw away
discarded dreams
scattered hopes.

will I be okay?

imminent death
slowly decaying
body of filth

disheveled man
lonely yet surrounded
hardened by pride
nowhere to hide

destruction is close
self mutilation
bloody lines
carving flesh
inches of pain
razor sharp
stinging showers
stained water
down the drain
the sound of silence.

cracked crystal ball

difficult planning

apparent desire
suicidal ideation
hopeless thoughts
