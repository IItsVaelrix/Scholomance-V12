Design — Revised: Foundry Base + GPU Effects

PixelBrain foundry renders the pixel art. A single effects shader adds school glow, rim light, and aura on top. No SDF body — the pixels are already good.

character-effects.frag.glsl — three techniques

Effect GLSL technique Uniforms
Eye glow Match pixels near u_eyeColor ± threshold; bloom into adjacent transparent pixels with school color u_schoolGlow, u_eyeColor, u_glowIntensity
Rim light Silhouette: opaque pixel whose left/top neighbour is transparent → tint with rim color at full alpha u_rimColor, u_glowIntensity
Foot aura Radial school-color gradient from bottom-centre of texture; blended only into transparent pixels u_schoolGlow, u_atmosphereOpacity

Key input: uniform sampler2D u_texture — the DynamicTexture the foundry painted. Shader only adds effects, never touches body shape.

BytecodeCompiler — now effects-only (5 uniforms, not 13)

Derives u_schoolGlow, u_rimColor, u_eyeColor, u_glowIntensity, u_atmosphereOpacity from spec.combatProfile.school + spec.materials.eyes. Pure function, same determinism guarantee. Simpler scope than the original 13-uniform body contract.
