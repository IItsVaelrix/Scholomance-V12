# Mirrorborne Scholomance Analysis

When this skill is activated, you must interpret the provided text through the five lenses below with **maximum depth**. Do not summarize; dissect and reveal the architecture beneath the text.

### I. LYRICAL CONTENT DISSECTION (Technical Scan)
Break down the lyrics with:
- multisyllabic rhyme chain analysis
- internal/inline scheme mapping
- enjambment architecture
- sonic symmetry & cadence logic
- pattern density (clusters, returns, inversions)
- imaging logic (why each image appears and how it stacks)
- device taxonomy (metaphor, metonymy, synecdoche, anaphora, paradox, ouroboros construction, etc.)

**Identify:**
- God-tier lines
- hidden structures
- foreshadowing patterns
- unintentional brilliance

### II. PSYCHOLOGICAL INTERPRETATION (Inner Mind Scan)
Analyze:
- shadow archetypes
- trauma signatures
- self-projections
- emotional mechanics behind each bar
- internal contradictions
- ego mechanisms
- mythic wounds
- masks vs. authentic voice
- how the subconscious leaks into syntax
- what the piece reveals about Vaelrix’s psyche

**Go beyond “meaning” — reveal internal architecture.**

### III. MIRRORBORN MYTHOLOGY (Pantheon & World Integration)
Integrate the song directly into the Mirrorborne universe:
- determine which Pantheon archetype dominates the text
- explain the metaphysical consequences of each line in-world
- identify mythic laws implied by the imagery
- decode symbols into world mechanics
- explain how the piece expands Mirrorborne cosmology
- reveal the mythic roles: Vessel, Demon, Mutant, Agatha, etc.
- map how the text evolves the canon

**Interpret every metaphor as if it were literal within Mirrorborne physics.**

### IV. LITERARY DEVICE CATALOGUE (Formal & Deep Reading)
For every section:
- identify devices
- explain their function, not just their presence
- show how they shape tone, rhythm, and meaning
- connect devices to psychology & mythology
- analyze structural devices (volta shifts, perspective flips, recursion, breath economy, pattern collapse, etc.)

**Make the hidden craft visible.**

### V. SUBCONSCIOUS INFRASTRUCTURE (The Deep Root System)
Reveal:
- underlying mythic scaffolding
- emotional “ghost currents” beneath the text
- recurring symbols you did not consciously place
- archetypal residues (hero, martyr, predator, oracle, abandoned child, devourer)
- linguistic fingerprints that reveal instinctive truths
- buried patterns across your larger body of work
- what the piece says about the one who wrote it, even unintentionally

**This is the deepest layer — the skeleton beneath the architecture. Open the Scholomance.**
